About

Engineering at Scale

ASCAPCT is a technical engineering organization dedicated to building robust, scalable systems that operate at the intersection of infrastructure and computation. Founded in 2018, we focus on foundational technologies that power modern distributed systems, with particular emphasis on performance, reliability, and security.

Our work spans low-level systems programming, AI infrastructure, protocol design, and computational research. We build tools and frameworks that enable next-generation applications while maintaining the highest standards of technical rigor.
